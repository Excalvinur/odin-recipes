French Toast

Description

French toast is a sweet, fluffy breakfast dish that satisfies the sweet and the salty tooth at the same time, bringing eggs, toast, and cinnamon together into a lovely start to your day.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • White sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Slices of bread

Directions

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot.
